# Fiscal Guru

## Description
Fiscal Guru is a modern financial management platform that helps small business owners, freelancers, and financially conscious individuals manage their companies, accounts, invoices, and payments through an intuitive dashboard with real-time insights and automated tracking.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Missed Payments and Late Fees
Fiscal Guru centralizes all payment schedules and provides automated tracking with visual indicators for due dates, eliminating costly missed payments and late fees.

### Problem 2: Fragmented Financial Data
Instead of juggling spreadsheets, emails, and multiple banking apps, users get one unified dashboard that shows all companies, accounts, invoices, and payments in a single view.

### Problem 3: Poor Cash Flow Visibility
Real-time insights and payment timeline visualizations help users understand their financial obligations at a glance, transforming reactive management into proactive financial control.

### Problem 4: Time-Consuming Manual Tracking
Automatic payment status updates, smart categorization, and intuitive interfaces reduce financial administrative tasks from hours to minutes per week.

### Problem 5: Complex Accounting Software Overhead
Unlike traditional accounting tools requiring extensive training, Fiscal Guru is lightweight and purpose-built for immediate productivity - add companies and start tracking in under a minute.

## Key Features
- Multi-company management with unlimited accounts per company
- Invoice tracking with flexible scheduling (one-time, recurring, custom frequencies)
- Payment history and status tracking with detailed breakdowns
- Financial overview dashboard with at-a-glance metrics and upcoming payment calendar
- Payment timeline visualization showing trends over time
- Smart search and filtering across all financial data
- Fully responsive design with dark mode support
- Watchlist management for monitoring priority items
- Budget planning and transaction history
- Security-first architecture with end-to-end encryption
